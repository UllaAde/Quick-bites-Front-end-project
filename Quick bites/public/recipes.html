Savoiardi cake with pineapples

Easy cake

Whip the sour cream with the sugar and powdered sugar. Dip the cookies in the cream. Arrange them in a single layer in the pan. Top with pineapple chunks and dried fruit, then another layer of cookies with sour cream, placing them perpendicular to the first layer. Spread the remaining cream over the cake on all sides and sprinkle with chocolate chips.

Ingredients:  500 grams of sour cream, 1 can of canned pineapple, Powdered sugar, Chocolate chips, Dried fruit, 200 g of savoiardi cookies

Christmas cookies "Piparkukas"

Cookies

Melt the butter in a saucepan, then add the honey, sugar, and salt. Continue heating, stirring constantly, until the sugar dissolves. Then add the gingerbread spices. Cool the contents of the saucepan for 15 minutes and pour into a bowl. Add the egg, sour cream, and baking powder. Gradually add the flour to make mixing easier. Refrigerate for about an hour. Roll out the dough to a thickness of about 3 mm. Use cookie cutters to shape the gingerbread cookies. Transfer them to a parchment-lined baking sheet. Bake in a preheated oven at 180°C (350°F). Small cookies will bake in 8 minutes, while larger ones may take 10-12 minutes.

Ingredients: 500 g wheat flour, 300 g honey, 100 g sugar, 100 g sour cream, 100 g butter, 10-20 g gingerbread spices, 30 g baking powder, 1 egg, a pinch of salt, and, of course, molds.



Hotcakes

Pancakes

In a deep bowl, combine the eggs, sugar, and vanilla. Beat with a whisk or mixer until lightly foamy. Pour in the milk and mix. Sift the flour and baking powder through a fine-mesh sieve. Mix well. Pour about 2 tablespoons of batter into the pan, forming a round pancake. Fry the hotcake over medium heat, one side at a time, for 2-3 minutes, until the top is opaque and bubbles appear on the surface. Then flip the hotcake and cook for another 1-2 minutes you can cover the pan with a lid. Fry the remaining hotcakes in the same manner.

Ingredients: Milk - 200 ml, eggs - 2 pcs, sugar - 80 g, wheat flour - 240 g (1.5 cups with a volume of 250 ml), baking powder - 7 g, vanillin - 1 pinch or vanilla sugar - 1-2 teaspoons
